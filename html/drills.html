Chord Drills

Here are various drills to help you improve your chord playing.

One Minute Changes

Pick two chords, or a progression of chords. When you press start, a 1 minute timer begins. Your goal is to play as many clean chord changes as you can in that time span. One "chord change" should count as when you land on the original chord. If you have two chords, A and B, one change is A B A. Aim for 25-30 changes (for two chords), or whenever you can change cleanly without thinking about it.
